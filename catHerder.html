Cat Herder

I am the lead at my part-time job and when I took over this position, I was surprised by how difficult making the schedule was.We only made one weekly schedule for the school term, however there was no website or app that I could use to easily request everyone’s availability and also to be able easily read this information. Making the schedule isn’t hard it’s getting the information and being able to read that is difficult. So, I decided to make the prototype for a scheduling app.
